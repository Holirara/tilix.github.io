The following guidelines are designed to support a polished and consistent brand image. These guidelines should be closely followed for all marketing assets and materials.

Logos

When incorporating the logo in communication material it is important that the logo is placed in a way that enables the logo to be surrounded with adequate space on all sides.

Depending on the context in which the logos will be placed in, there are two options to choose from depending on the color of the background where the logo will be set. It is essential that the logo that will provide the most contrast with the background be selected to assure readability.

Which font used for the Tilix logo?
For Tilix logo, Arvo font is used..

Color Palette

The primary color palette comprises of three central colors that have been adopted as the signature colors for the ONESELECT brand. These colors are purple, green and dark grey.

Light gray should be applied as background for the content blocks or individual design elements to further create visual structure.

What are best practices for capturing our photographic style?

  • Capture authentic moments; show people at ease and content rather than posed, staged or overly cheerful.
  • Allow little imperfections or “happenings” such as glare from a window, a slightly messy desk or someone in mid-thought.
  • Show people’s real-life use of our products; allow product to provide context for the story without always being the focus of the shot.
  • Use our event images for our website.
  • Avoid digitally enhanced or complex, photo-montaged imagery.

Where to find great images

Powerful imagery comes from good photographers and illustrators. You can source good images from commercial image banks (eg 123RF or istockphoto) or from recommended photographers/illustrators or from the growing Tilix’s image library.
Authentic photography is our long-term goal, but at times it will be necessary to purchase stock photography.

A good photographer/illustrator takes a brief and invests time into planning the shot and interpreting that brief to the best effect.
